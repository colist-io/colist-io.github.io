How It Works

1

Find or Offer a Task

Browse community needs in your area or post your own micro-job. From trash pickup to local gigs, find work that matters.

2

Complete the Work

Do the physical work and document it with photos, timestamps, and geotags for verification.

3

Have the Other Party Verify

When the other party submits photos from their account confirming the work that was done it can be minted into immutable proof.

4

Earn $CLT Tokens

Receive $CLT tokens as proof of physical work completion and exchange this utility token for community service or other rewards.
